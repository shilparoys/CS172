Financial Aid Deadline

If you wish to apply for scholarship, loan and job assistance from MIT, please submit  the required documents by February 15, 2015 for both early-action and regular-action admissions applicants.

Life @ MIT

The Experimental Study Group (ESG), encourages students and teaching staff to get to know each other, and enables instructors to adapt their pacing and teaching to be nimble and responsive to their students’ needs. To find out more about the ESG Freshman Living Community, visit the wiki.
